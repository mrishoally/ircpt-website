About us

THE INTERRELIGIOUS COUNCIL FOR PEACE TANZANIA(IRCPT) is an interfaith organization made up of a member Faith Based organization ,for the purpose of enhancing Peace and peaceful coexistence through constructive dialogue and collaborations . founded and got its first registered in1992 as the peace journey Trust . (GPJ) Founder member were three women peace activists from Christians and Muslim faith. In 1996 religious leaders agreed to establish an interreligious council and an become an Affiliate of the world conference on Religions for peace international (WCRPT), now known as Religious for peace (Rfp).

IRCPT as many other Non governmental organizations was required to re-register under the NGO Act 2002 ; therefore on the 18th July 2019 IRCPT was registered No,NGO/R2/00099

Approaches

  • Strenghthening Unity and Cohesion
    Among faith based organizations
  • Established and formed interfaith committees
    In different Regions and Districts.
  • Establishment of community based financial groups
    popularly known as village community Bank (VICOBA)
  • Development of training manual and publications
    On different activities as advocacy and reference materials
  • reaching of consensus
    Contributed in part to reaching of consensus (Mwafaka) 2001 IN ZANZIBAR
  • 01.Dialogue
    We encourage dialogue for conflict resolutions ,reconciliations and rebuilding relationships.
  • 02.Visiting
    We organize an interfaith visiting to the faith based organizations , institutions and worshiping houses for learning and networking
  • 03.Training and seminars
    We conduct capacity building training and seminars that equip religious leaders ,women and youth of faith knowledge and skills of developing countering narratives in prevention of violent extremism (PVE) , explore early warnings conflict prevention and feedback mechanism as a measure of conflict preventation.
  • 04.Sports and games
    We are mobilize religious leaders and to organize football matches ,games and peace marathons for friendship and brotherhood.
  • 05.Conferences
    We are having annually interfaith conferences
  • 06.Caravans
    We are organize interfaith peace caravans demonstrations for currently topic and agenda.
  • Faith Based Approaches
    advocating for change based on religious teachings and moral principles
  • Right Based Approaches
    involve legal advocacy, policy reform and awareness campaigns
